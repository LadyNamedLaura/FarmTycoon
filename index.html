    Welcome to FarmTycoon

    this is the website of FarmTycoon, a school project which has gotten out of control.

    What is FarmTycoon?

    FarmTycoon is a farm simulating game written in java.

    Who made FarmTycoon?

    Simon Peeters, Laurens Van Damme, Riges De Witte and Barny Pieters made FarmTycoon in the beginning of 2012.

    Why was FarmTycoon made?

    FarmTycoon is the result of a school project during the second term of the first year of bachelor in applied informatics at HogeschoolGent (hogent.be)
